<p><strong>The Thermal Dilatometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitance Thermal Dilatometer</li><li>Connecting Rod (Push Rod) Thermal Dilatometer</li><li>Optical Thermal Dilatometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal dilatometers market comprises various types of devices used to measure thermal expansion of materials. Capacitance thermal dilatometers utilize changes in capacitance due to material expansion, offering high precision for small samples. Connecting rod (push rod) thermal dilatometers employ a mechanical rod to transfer displacement measurements, suitable for larger samples. Optical thermal dilatometers use laser or optical methods to measure length changes, providing non-contact measurements that enhance accuracy and applicability in various research and industrial settings.</p></p>
