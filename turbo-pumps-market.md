<p><p>Turbo pumps are categorized into several types based on their lubrication and support mechanisms. Oil-lubricated turbo pumps use a traditional lubrication system to reduce friction and wear, ensuring efficient operation, particularly in industrial applications. Magnetically suspended turbo pumps utilize magnetic levitation technology to eliminate physical contact between components, reducing friction and improving reliability and efficiency, especially in cryogenic applications. Additionally, "Others" encompasses hybrid or specialized designs that may combine features of both types or serve niche markets with specific requirements.</p></p>
