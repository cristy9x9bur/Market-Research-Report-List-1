<p><p>The Sedan and Hatchback Wheel Speed Sensor market is primarily categorized into Hall Effect and Magnetic Electric types. Hall Effect sensors utilize a semiconductor to detect magnetic fields, providing precise speed readings essential for advanced vehicle dynamics and stability control. Conversely, Magnetic Electric sensors operate based on the principle of electromagnetic induction, generating voltage proportional to the wheel's rotational speed. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety and performance through accurate monitoring of wheel speeds in modern automobiles.</p></p>
