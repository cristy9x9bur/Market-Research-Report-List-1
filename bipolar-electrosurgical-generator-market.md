<p><p>The Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator is a medical device that utilizes high-frequency electrical currents to cut tissue and coagulate blood during surgical procedures. Unlike monopolar generators, which use a single electrode, bipolar systems have both the active and return electrodes integrated into one instrument, improving precision and minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. This functionality enhances its applications in various surgical specialties, including gynecology, urology, and neurosurgery.</p><p>The Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period.
