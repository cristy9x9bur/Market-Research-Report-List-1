<p><p>Automatic flow wrapping machines are categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal flow wrappers operate with products moving horizontally, ideal for items with a stable shape, ensuring a tight seal and efficient packaging. Conversely, vertical flow wrappers handle products that are fed vertically, making them suitable for bulk or loose items like granules and powders. Both types enhance packaging efficiency, reduce manual labor, and ensure consistent quality, catering to diverse industry needs in food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>
