<p><strong>The Invasive Blood Glucose Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GDH-FAD</li><li>GOD</li><li>Electrochemistry</li><li>GDH</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The invasive blood glucose meter market consists of several types based on the enzymatic methods used for glucose detection. GDH-FAD (glucose dehydrogenase flavin adenine dinucleotide) is recognized for its accuracy and reduced interference from substances like maltose. GOD (glucose oxidase) relies on oxygen consumption and is widely used but can be affected by other sugars. Electrochemistry employs various technologies for precise measurement. GDH (glucose dehydrogenase) is another biochemical method, while "Other" includes emerging technologies that may not fit existing categories.</p></p>
