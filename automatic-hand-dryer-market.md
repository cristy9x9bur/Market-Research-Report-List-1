<p><strong>The Automatic Hand Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Air Dryer</li><li>Jet Air Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic hand dryer market is primarily classified into two types: hot air dryers and jet air dryers. Hot air dryers use warm air to evaporate moisture from hands, taking longer to dry compared to other methods. In contrast, jet air dryers utilize high-velocity air streams to quickly remove water, making them more efficient for rapid hand drying. Both types aim to enhance hygiene, reduce paper waste, and offer a cost-effective solution for public restrooms, with varying energy consumption and drying times.</p></p>
