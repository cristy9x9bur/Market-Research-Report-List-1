<p><p>The USB Type-C adapters market encompasses two main types: USB-C Adapter Converters and USB-C Multiport Adapters. USB-C Adapter Converters are designed to convert a USB-C connection to other formats, such as USB-A or HDMI, facilitating compatibility between devices. In contrast, USB-C Multiport Adapters combine multiple ports into a single device, allowing users to connect various peripherals, such as USB-C, HDMI, Ethernet, and SD card readers, enhancing connectivity options for laptops and smartphones.</p></p>
