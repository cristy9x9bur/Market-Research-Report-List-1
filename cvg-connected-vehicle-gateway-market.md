<p><p>Connected Vehicle Gateway (CVG) refers to a system facilitating communication between vehicles and external networks. The Central Gateway acts as the main point for data management and communication, integrating various vehicle systems and ensuring efficient connectivity. Meanwhile, the LAN Gateway connects in-vehicle networks, such as infotainment and telemetry systems, to other external networks, optimizing data transfer and enhancing vehicle functionality. Both types are crucial for ensuring seamless communication, improving safety, and enabling telematics services in connected vehicles.</p></p>
